# Server Rules

**POLICY:**

1. **Manners:** Any form of harassments , discrimination, toxic, and other unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated by any means. Always treat you fellow players with respect as we are building a friendly and non-toxic community.
2. **Real Money Trading (RMT):**  Selling items or zeny for real money is **permitted**. However, the server/team assumes no liability for any item losses arising from scams or fraudulent transactions.
3. **Gameplay:** We highly encourage everyone to play fair for a quality gaming experience. Any form of cheating, botting, or bug exploiting will not be tolerated.&#x20;
4. **GM Staff:** Game Masters will not initiate private messages or request login credentials. Any GM impersonation will result in a permanent ban. Please notify staff immediately if such an event occurs.
5. **Advertisement:** Advertising or promoting other games, private servers, or websites is strictly prohibited.
